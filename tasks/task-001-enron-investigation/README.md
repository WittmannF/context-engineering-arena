# The Enron Investigation Brief

**Benchmark question:** What warning signs, coordination patterns, or internal tensions were visible before the collapse, and what evidence supports those conclusions?

This task includes sample and full-mode scripts, an answer schema, and a rubric. Raw data is not committed.

Required sections:

- Executive Summary
- Investigative Timeline
- Key People and Communication Patterns
- Main Themes and Topic Clusters
- Evidence-backed Claims
- Contradictions and Weak Signals
- Documents or Messages That Mattered Most
- What Was Ignored and Why
- Context Engineering Strategy
- Ethical and Evidentiary Limitations
