# SEC Risk Radar

**Benchmark question:** How did the company’s disclosed risks, strategic concerns, and business narrative change over time, and which changes would a careful analyst notice?

This task includes sample and full-mode scripts, an answer schema, and a rubric. Raw data is not committed.

Required sections:

- Executive Summary
- Company and Filing Scope
- Timeline of Filings
- Risk Factor Evolution
- Language Drift
- Newly Introduced Risks
- Softened or Removed Risks
- Evidence-backed Claims
- Analyst Notes and Open Questions
- Context Engineering Strategy
